Perrin and their trusty assistant Carrie delve into the gas-filled mines in search of The Stone. Use Carrie's... unique abilities to search for and avoid deadly gas as you explore the cave, mining the various ores found around to rack up a score. Will you be able to uncover the legendary Stone?

This game was made for the 2025 Kaboom's Backlog game jam at Bradley. Because this game jam was a low-stakes environment, I and three other programming-focused friends decided to work as a team of only programmers. Despite our lack of apparent skill diversity, we all managed to utilize our secondary skills to make the game's assets entirely in-house.
