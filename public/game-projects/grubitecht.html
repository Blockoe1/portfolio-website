Grubitecht

Defend your village from incoming insect invaders by moving buildings in this 3D grid-based RTS.

Your village is under attack by a legion of insect invaders! Play as a group of grub architects determined to help their village by moving various defensive structures around to adapt the town's defenses to incoming waves of enemies. Will your efforts allow the villagers to see another day?

This project was made for my Intro to Game Scripting class at Bradley.


My Contributions:

  • Developed a voxel-style level building tool based on the Unity Tilemap system.
  • Utilized polymorphism to create adaptable structure UI displays.
  • Implemented A* pathfinding algorithms for enemy movement.
  • Balanced enemy waves based on player feedback.
  • Coded unique enemy and structure behaviors.
