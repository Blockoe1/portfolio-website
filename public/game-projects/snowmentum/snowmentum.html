Build up your Snowmentum, destroy everything in your path, and leave snow-body unscathed!  In Snowmentum, you play as a snowball as you roll down a mountain slope and destroy as much of the environment as you can.  As your snowball grows while rolling down the hill, previously impassable obstacles become easily crushed.  Destroy objects to gain points, and try to rack up as high a score as you can!

This project was made over the fall 2025 semester for our Game Production I class at Bradley as part of a contract with the 8-Bit Beercade.  Over the course of the semester, I worked with a team of 5 to create this snowy arcade infinite runner that now is available for 8-Bit patrons to enjoy.
