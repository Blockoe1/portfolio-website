What is eggception but an egg within an egg within an egg...?

In this boss rush, you must fight your way out and eggscape from the confines of the outermost shell. Along the way, you will face great dangers. Fight eggceptionally powerful foes by flinging eggs or smacking with your frying pan while dodging enemy eggtacks. If your shell gets fried, your movement will become much smoother to help you eggvade. Your spirit may crack under the pressure. Your brain may become scrambled. But, if you eggscape, you will find a hidden truth.

This game was made for the 2026 Crossplay Game Jam, a collaborative game jam between Bradley University and 8 other universities across the midwest, with the theme of "You can't make an omlette without breaking a few eggs." Since we had a team of 10, we focused on creating the most polished game we could within our time limit.

My Contributions:

  • Developed a state machine based boss-making tool that allowed designers to easily make changes to boss behaviors.
  • Programmed boss movement, attack, and health systems.
  • Collaborated with a teammate to implement the first boss, the Mixing Bowl, based on their design specifics.
  • Utilized ProBuilder to create a screen shatter transition effect that shows breaking through each egg layer.
  • Added polish effects to player attacks, such as a swing trail and screenshake.
  • Assisted with playtesting and balancing bosses based on playtester feedback.
  • Enhanced player communication by implementing telegraphing for boss attacks.
