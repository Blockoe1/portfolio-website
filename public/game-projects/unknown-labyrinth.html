"Oh no! The adventurers are coming, but my dungeon isn't prepared yet! I guess I'll just have to build it as they explore."" In Unknown Labyrinth, one player plays as an evil Lich working to create a dungeon by placing room cards that the remaining three players, the adventurers, explore. Each adventurer has their own attributes that will help them conquer the challenges the Lich presents them. If the adventurers manage to defeat one of the dungeon's bosses, then they will be victorious! But if they fall, they will be lost to the Lich's labyrinth for eternity.

This project was made during the Spring 2025 semester for Game Design I class at Bradley with the restriction that the game we made had to fit inside of a mint tin. For many of us, this was our first time working in a team, but my team of four worked hard to fit an ambitious dungeon game into tiny box space allowed.
