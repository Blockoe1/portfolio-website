Skydive after a cheeseburger in this two-player versus party game prototype! You're both falling through the sky, competing for who gets the most bites out of that juicy burger. Make contact with the burger to grab it and start scoring, but beware if you're hit by an obstacle or the other player's slaps it'll be knocked out of your hands for your opponent to nab. Reach the ground with the most points to claim sweet meaty victory!

This project was made as a rapid prototype during the Spring 2026 semester for Game Design II class at Bradley. For this project, we were given a prompt of three random words that led to the crazy premise: Jumbo, Skydive, Cheeseburger.

Because this was a prototype, we were also challenged to try something new for this project, so we decided to attempt implementing gyroscope controls as a technical and design challenge. It was my responsibility as main programmer to figure out how to read gyroscope input and setup movement controls based around that input.

My Contributions:

  • Setup systems to read gyroscope input from Nintendo Switch Pro Controllers.
  • Iterated on four different movement systems to make gyroscope input feel good to use.
  • Coordinated playtesting to gather feedback on gryoscope controls and player interactions.
  • Programmed main player mechanics like slapping and grabbing the burger.
  • Setup UI code to display players position relative to each other.
